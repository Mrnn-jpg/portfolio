about me

Hi, my name is Marianna and I’m a self-taught artist and illustrator from Lviv, Ukraine. My passion is to create illustrations that fascinate both children and adults. Getting inspired by other artists, fantasy books and everyday life. I really love watching my imagination flow when i read stories where reality meets magic and mystery, and feel an uncontrollable desire to recreate everything on paper and create an atmospheric illustration.
I would love to work on projects that will inspire others to be creative and happy. I can work in different styles and adapt to any requiements. I am ready to work, gain more experience and acquire new skills . I’m always open to challenges and new ideas and ready to learn new things to become even better as an illustrator.
I mostly work with Procreate, but i also have knowledge and skills to use Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

experience

I gained most of my experience as a freelancer. It was postcards for a coffee shop, illustrations for clothes, cups and plates. Also I worked with individual customers requests and university projects.

education

Finishing Bachelor's Degree at Lviv Polytechnic National University - Institute of Architecture and Design.
Completed such online courses as: Sketchbook Techniques for Children's Illustration by Ema Malyauka, Illustrating Fantastical Animals with Procreate by Julia Körner, Atmospheric Scenes in Procreate: Paint with Color and Light by Ramona Wultschner, Character Creation from Observational Drawings by Sarah van Dongen, Pictorial Sketchbook with Gouache by Maru Godas, Introduction to Adobe Illustrator by Aarón Martínez,Daily Sketching for Creative Inspiration by Sorie Kim, Exploratory Sketchbook: Find Your Drawing Style by Sarah van Dongen, Creative Drawing Techniques for Beginners by Puño, Adobe Illustrator for Advanced Illustration by Aarón Martínez.
